Looking to stay up to date with the latest teaching and learning opportunities from the SAP University Alliances program? Join our Global Knowledge Sharing Session - the Quarterly Webinars designed to keep educators and researchers connected with the latest developments across SAP, academia, and the broader SAP ecosystem. What to Expect Each webinar includes: 40–45 minutes of presentations, updates, and community sharing 10–15 minutes of interactive Q&A To accommodate our global community, each session is offered in two time slots : EMEA/APAC , and AMER/EMEA.
