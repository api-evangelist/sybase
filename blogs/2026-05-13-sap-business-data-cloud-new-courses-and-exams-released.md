To support the development of your SAP BDC skills we offer a learning journey that comprises a series of courses and ends with a certification. The SAP BDC learning journey was first released in the spring of 2025 and the courses it contains have undergone incremental adjustments as new features were added. But things move fast in the land of SAP BDC and so for the latest update, we decided not to simply adjust and add more content to the existing courses, but to completely rewrite them!
